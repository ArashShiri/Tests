Format Information

EXR (OpenEXR)

  • Industry standard for VFX
  • Supports HDR and floating-point
  • Excellent compression ratios
  • Supports multiple channels

TIFF

  • Widely supported format
  • Good for photography
  • Variable compression options
  • Cross-platform compatibility

DPX

  • Digital cinema standard
  • Film and broadcast industry
  • Logarithmic color encoding
  • Minimal compression
Note: Calculated bitrates are estimates. Actual file sizes may vary based on image content, compression efficiency, and metadata. For video sequences, multiply by frame rate to get bitrate per second.
